Description

Dallas has the third highest childhood poverty rate among large US cities. About 1/3 children in Dallas live in poverty. - Dallas Mayor's Task Force on Poverty, September 2017 by KERANews. According to the Children’s Defense Fund, nearly one in five or 13.2 million children in the U.S. are poor, more than 1.2 million public school children are homeless. Scientist are now studying the effects of poverty on children's brain development. Research indicates that cognitive development is closely linked to socio-economic status. These effects impact cognitive performance both intellectually and emotionally. The gap starts in early childhood and continues throught brain development.
